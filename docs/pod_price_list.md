# Pod Cost Price List — High-Level Working Budget

**Version:** Draft pricing register  
**Date:** 2026-05-03  
**Currency:** EUR unless noted otherwise  
**Use:** Internal feasibility / prototype cost planning for timber pod manufacturing.

## Important Notes

This price list is a **working estimate**, not a supplier quotation.

Prices are based on the high-level figures discussed for Latvian / nearby retail and small-trade purchasing. They should be replaced with live supplier prices once the material price database is added to the app.

The current agreed commercial direction is:

- **Concrete / groundworks:** separate extra
- **Roof finish:** EPDM roof system
- **Heating:** electric radiators
- **Ventilation:** trickle vents + bathroom/kitchen extract where required
- **MVHR:** not standard; future premium option only
- **CCTV:** optional CAT6/IP camera provision
- **PV-ready roof:** optional provision
- **Standard wall direction:** mineral wool between studs + 70mm PIR outboard
- **Premium wall direction:** PIR-heavy wall retained as enhanced option

# 3. Wall Build-Up Options

## 3.1 Recommended Standard Hybrid Wall

**Use as default commercial wall.**

Inside → outside:

1. Plasterboard 12.5mm  
2. Service void 25–50mm  
3. Standard VCL  
4. OSB/3 11–18mm  
5. C24 framing zone + mineral wool infill 140–150mm  
6. PIR outboard 70mm  
7. Breather membrane  
8. Ventilated cavity / battens 25mm  
9. Timber or fibre cement cladding  

| Item | Typical Quantity Basis | Rate Basis | Notes |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| Mineral wool between studs | net wall area × 85% | €7–€8.50/m² | Replaces PIR infill |
| PIR outboard 70mm | net wall area | €18–€20/m² | Main thermal upgrade |
| C24 framing | calculated lm | €3.00–€3.70/lm | Studs + plates + noggins |
| Battens | calculated lm | €0.60–€0.80/lm | Service/cavity |
| Approx U-value | — | ~0.153–0.159 W/m²K | Depending OSB/stud depth/service void |

**Commercial position:** best default balance of performance and cost.

## 10.3 Supplier Strategy

For first prototype:

- use Latvian suppliers where practical
- keep pricing simple and local

After 3–5 real orders:

- RFQ Poland, Lithuania, Estonia, Ukraine if logistics acceptable
- target mineral wool, PIR, cladding, windows, doors, OSB, membranes
- negotiate pallet/truckload pricing

Europages can be used as a **supplier discovery tool**, not final price truth.
